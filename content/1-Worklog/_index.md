During this graduation internship, I had the privilege of interning at a training partner company of Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam. I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the company and my mentor, Mr. Nguyen Gia Hung, for their guidance, mentorship, and support throughout my learning and working journey.

I started my internship on April 17, 2026, as a Cloud Engineer Intern, completing a 3-month tenure that concluded on July 30, 2026. Throughout this period, I gained hands-on experience with core AWS cloud services (such as IAM, VPC, EC2, S3, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, Cognito, CloudFront, WAF, CloudWatch, and Bedrock), while also learning how to design architectures following the principles of the AWS Well-Architected Framework.

To build our practical project and achieve the best results, I worked closely with my team members: Dang Quoc Tuan, Nguyen Duy Phuong, and Ho Xuan Nguyen. This collaboration helped us enhance our teamwork skills, technical communication, and overall mastery of cloud computing technologies.
